A **business document apostille** makes Indian corporate papers valid in **Hague Convention** countries.  
**VerifiedApostille** helps Indian companies, founders, and NRIs complete the **Notary → CoC → MEA** chain quickly, with **worldwide courier delivery**.

---

## Sticker vs e-Apostille (QR)

- **Sticker Apostille (physical):** MEA sticker affixed to the document—often required for POA, MOA/AOA, Incorporation.  
- **e-Apostille (QR):** Digital apostille with QR verification—fast and shareable, frequently accepted for **invoices/commercial docs**.

- **POA / MOA / AOA / Incorporation:** typically require **originals** for Sticker routes.
